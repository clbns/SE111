Synopsis

The Last of the Mohicans tells the story of three peaceful, solitary trappers who live alongside the British colonists trying to survive in the English Colonies of America in 1757, when they stumble upon a British colonel's daughters being kidnapped by a traitorous scout and decide they have to risk their lives and their freedom to rescue the siblings in the midst of a military conflict they wanted no part in: the French and Indian War.
